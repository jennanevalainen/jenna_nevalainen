You can apply to HAMK’s degree programmes conducted in English in the joint application to higher education in January. The joint application closes on 19 January 2022 3:00 pm (UTC +2). The application form is available at www.studyinfo.fi.

In Bachelor’s degree programmes student selection will be made based on International UAS Exam entrance examination, certificate-based selection based on matriculation examination and SAT test results. In Smart and Sustainable Design degree programmes student selection will be made based on HAMK’s own entrance examination.

International UAS Exam consists of two phases organized remotely online. First phase: written examination is organized on 9 February 2022 at 12:00 (UTC/GMT +2). All eligible applicants who have passed the written exam will be invited to the second phase. Second phase: group interviews are organized between 2 – 16 March 2022.
